# 8.¬†Try Home Circuit Training

Circuit training is an excellent way to burn fat and build muscle in a short period. Create a circuit by choosing 4-6 exercises (such as push-ups, jumping jacks, planks, and squats) and repeat them for 30 seconds to 1 minute each. Take a short break between each exercise, and repeat the circuit 1‚Äì3 times based on your energy level. This way, you can keep your workout intense and effective without any fancy equipment.
