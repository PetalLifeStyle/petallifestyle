## What Even Is a Toner Pad?

A toner pad is basically a pre-soaked cotton pad that does multiple jobs at once: it tones, exfoliates (lightly), hydrates, and preps your skin for the rest of your routine - all in one swipe. Think of it as the lazy genius of skincare. No cotton ball, no pouring, no mess.

They slot in right after cleansing, before serums and moisturizer. ## How to Actually Use Toner Pads (Don't Skip This Part)

1. **Clean face first.** Toner pads go on after your cleanser, not instead of it.
2. **Swipe gently.** Don't scrub. These aren't makeup remover wipes.
3. **For double-sided pads:** use the textured/exfoliating side first, then flip for the hydrating side.
4. **Let it fully absorb** before applying serums or moisturizer - usually 30 to 60 seconds.
5. **Finish with SPF in the morning.** Not optional. Check [The Best Korean Sunscreens Based On Skin Type](https://petallifestyle.pages.dev/posts/the-best-korean-sunscreens-based-on-skin-type/)for options that won't pill or feel heavy.
6. **Don't layer exfoliating pads** (like Medicube) with other acids or exfoliants the same day.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can toner pads replace my regular toner?**

For most people, yes - especially hydrating and soothing pads. Exfoliating pads (like Medicube) also replace chemical toners. You don't need to use both.

**How often should I use toner pads?**

Hydrating/soothing pads: daily, morning and/or evening. Exfoliating pads: start with every other day and see how your skin reacts. More is not better here.

**Can I use toner pads if I have sensitive skin?**

Yes, but pick the right ones. numbuzin Centella and BRING GREEN are both gentle enough. Avoid Medicube Zero Pore if your skin barrier is already compromised.

**Do toner pads work for dark spots or hyperpigmentation?**

Not directly. Exfoliating pads can help fade them faster by accelerating cell turnover, but they're not a targeted treatment for hyperpigmentation.

**Are toner pads wasteful?**

They do generate cotton waste. Some brands (like MEDIHEAL with vegan fiber) are better than others. You can also cut pads in half if the size is generous - a lot of people do this with the larger ones.

**Do toner pads expire after opening?**

Most have 6 to 12 months after opening. Write the date on the jar when you open it so you don't forget.

**Can men use toner pads?**

Skin doesn't have a gender. Yes.
